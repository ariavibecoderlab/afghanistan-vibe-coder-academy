## 1. Executive Summary

Afghanistan has one of the youngest populations on Earth — **over 60% under 25**. This is not a burden. It is a superpower waiting to be unlocked.

**Afghanistan Vibe Coder Academy** is a free, government-partnered online program that trains Afghan youth (ages 15–18) in modern AI-powered software development — what we call **"vibe coding"** — the ability to build real applications using AI tools, without needing years of traditional computer science education.

In **3 to 6 months**, participants go from zero to building functional web apps, mobile apps, and AI-powered tools — skills that are immediately monetizable in the global digital economy.

**No prior coding experience required. No university degree needed. Just a smartphone or laptop and internet.**

## 3. Program Design

### Target
- **Age:** 15–18 years old
- **Cost to students:** 100% FREE
- **Format:** 100% Online (mobile-friendly)
- **Duration:** 3 months (Core) → 6 months (Advanced)
- **Language:** English + Dari/Pashto support materials
- **Cohort Size:** 1,000 students per batch (scalable to 10,000+)

### Curriculum Overview

#### Phase 1: Foundation (Month 1)
- Digital literacy & internet navigation
- English for tech (essential vocabulary)
- Introduction to AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, Lovable)
- Building your first website in 1 day

#### Phase 2: Vibe Coding Core (Months 2–3)
- Prompt engineering — how to talk to AI to build software
- Building web applications with AI assistance
- Frontend development (HTML, CSS, React basics)
- Backend basics (Supabase, Firebase)
- Deploying real apps to the internet
- **Capstone Project:** Each student builds and launches a working app

#### Phase 3: Advanced & Monetization (Months 4–6)
- Full-stack application development
- AI integration (chatbots, automation, data tools)
- Mobile app development
- Freelancing on Upwork, Fiverr, Toptal
- Building SaaS products
- Islamic digital economy opportunities (Malakat ecosystem)
- **Final Project:** Build a product that generates income

### Delivery Method
- **Pre-recorded video lessons** (watch anytime, any speed)
- **Live weekly sessions** with instructors (Q&A, code-along)
- **Peer learning groups** via WhatsApp/Telegram
- **Mentorship** from global Muslim tech professionals
- **Project-based** — learn by building, not by watching
